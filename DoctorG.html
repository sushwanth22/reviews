DOCTOR G

Rating: ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Starring: Ayushmann Khurrana, Rakul Preet Singh and Shefali Shah
Directed by: Anubhuti Kashyap
Written by: Sumit Saxena, Saurabh Bharat, Vishal Wagh, Anubhuti Kashyap
Story by: Saurabh Bharat, Vishal Wagh
Produced by: Junglee Pictures
Music by: Ketan Sodha
Language: Hindi

My Review: Doctor G is a fun movie with emotional touch. Overall the movie is great but somehow you feel like they are dragging the movie to make it 2hrs+. I felt like movie completed within 1hr but again they went off topic and brought another problem about minor pregnancy. Even with the story flip it maintained its connect via emotions. Overall you can watch this movie for those who likes simple movie with fun and emotions.
