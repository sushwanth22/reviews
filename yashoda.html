YASHODA

Rating : ★★★☆☆ (3/5)

Starring: Samantha, Unni Mukundan, Varalaxmi Sarathkumar, Murali Sharma, Rao Ramesh
Directed & Written by: Hari and Harish
Produced by: Sivalenka Krishna Prasad
Music by: Mani Sharma
Language: Telugu

My Review: Samantha starring movie Yashoda starts with a good opening and kept it's best to mantain suspense. In some parts of the first half contains lag scenes but with the side characters it maintained it's grip. special mentions are sampath,varalaxmi & murali and beside these, others actors also did great. Overall movie is good but ending not that satisfactory. I felt that they should have improvised it. But overall you can watch it as timepass movie.
