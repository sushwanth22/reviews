DHAMAKA

Rating: ★★☆☆☆ (2/5)

Starring: Ravi Teja, Sree Leela, Rao Ramesh
Directed by: Trinadha Rao Nakkina
Written by: Prasanna Kumar Bezawada
Produced by: Abhishek Agarwal, T. G. Vishwa Prasad
Music by: Bheems Ceciroleo
Language: Telugu

My Review: Dhamaka is an intresting and routine commercial movie but interval twist is somewhat unexpected From starting to ending there are only fights and dance, but we got to witness vintage Ravi Teja comedy timing and energetic dance performance along actress Sree Leela. The story continues only on simple single path. Overall those who are fans of Ravi teja and commercial movies can enjoy the movie.
